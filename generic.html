My Journey

From writing my first lines of code at University in the context of Logistics and Supply Chain Management to my time in the professional work environment, I have always been fortunate enough to learn new tools and possible solutions. Being able to enhance and use my personal skillset and knowledge is a feeling I find great joy in.
I started with algorithms for heuristic solution methods in the context of Logistics, moved to learning Data Analysis tools such as SQL, AWS Quicksight and Tableau and advanced with exploratory Data Analysis in Python using libraries such as Pandas and Seaborn. Currently, I am dedicating myself to Machine Learning methods to expand my Data Analysis skills.
I specifically enjoy interacting with colleagues about trying to find the best solution together to further develop a business or product. My practical experience and the insights that I have gained through data help me with this.
